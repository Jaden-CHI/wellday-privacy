WellDay Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-01-01

WellDay ("the App") is committed to protecting users’ personal information and ensuring transparency. This Privacy Policy explains what information the WellDay app collects, how it is used, stored, and protected.

1. Information We Collect

WellDay collects the following information:

Information Entered Directly by Users

  • Water intake times and records
  • Medication schedules and records
  • Notification setting preferences

Device Information (Automatically Collected)

  • Operating system information (e.g., Android version)
  • App version
  • Device model

※ This information is used solely for app improvement, error analysis, and stability enhancement. It is not used for personal identification.

Notification Permission (Push Notification)

The App requests notification permission to send reminders for water intake and medication schedules.

2. Purpose of Information Use

Collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing water intake and medication reminders
  • Offering personalized tracking features
  • Improving performance and diagnosing issues

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • User-entered data is stored only on the device and is not transmitted externally.
  • Notification-related temporary data is deleted immediately when no longer required.

4. No Data Sharing

WellDay does not share, sell, or transfer any personal data to third parties under any circumstances.
